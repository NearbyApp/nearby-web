What is Nearby?

Nearby is a centralized app for Spotteds.

What can I do with Nearby?

You can share a live moment using a message from your location. You can also attach a picture to your spotted if you want.

What are public and private Spotted?

When you share a public Spotted, your name and profile picture from Google or Facebook will appear. If you share a private Spotted, then your Spotted will appear anonymously.
