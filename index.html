A centralize app for the Spotted's

Nearby is a mobile app that simplify the Spotted page on Facebook. You can now use this mobile app to share your Spotted message on our centralized system. Your message will be posted instantly and you have the option to share it anonymously.

Features

  • Share

    Sharing your Spotted is now so simple with Nearby. When you share your message, you don't have to wait for approval.

  • Around

    When you post something, you post it where you are. So, it come very easy to see where the message has been shared.

  • Anonymity

    With the Nearby app, you have the option to share your message anonymously. So, no one will know your identity. You can also share as your real identity.
